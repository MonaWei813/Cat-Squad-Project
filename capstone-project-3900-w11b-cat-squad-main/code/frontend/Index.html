About Wanted

Wanted is a free online job market. We offer three types of user account. For jobseekers, the account will help you to promote yourself and find your dream job. For employers, this website will offer a chance to recruit great employees. And for the agents, you can get access to both jobseekers and employers' information, and help them to connect to each other.
